Main Content

vision

It’s very dangerous that without hearing anything but riding the bike , and at the same time using the navigator will enhance the risk of having accident , because if you can’t hear anything you have to always look at your phone, however under the strong sunlight you may not be able to see anything on the phone. Hence in these conditions when deaf people are turning or stopping suddenly without knowing the condition around is very dangerous.

Under these considerations on the road, the BIKE FOR DEAF born. user can first tell the bike where you want to go, then the bike will setup the navigation, and tell you to turn through the vibration of your grips at the corresponding side. And from approach to turn , the vibration will get stronger and stronger, making sure that the user will turn suddenly. Through the sensor on the bike, it can distinguish coming car and people from your back where user can’t see but approaching and inside the safe region of the user, then the bike will interact with the user through the vibration of the seat and represent the corresponding sign on the smartphone screen. So the user can have a look to the back or on the mirror and pay more attention to identify what kind of reactions he\she need to do. In advance it will use the light and play some sounds to the environment to warn the others. Users can cancel the warning if the situation is safe but the system is keeping reminding, like in the traffic jam. So in this way even you can’t listen to anything for knowing the surroundings, you can still enjoy your riding safely !!

AMI steps

acting

the vibrating feedback from the grips, visual feedback of the light, and sound play.

interacting

user can control the vibration off or on, the phone will sent the feedback information for the user to let user be careful. the vibration will warn the user for the upcoming vehicle or people

sensing

the bike can sense the movement behind the user, the distance and speed

reasoning

through the sensor the bike can distinguish the vehicle and people, if they will cause dangerous for user, and figure out if light and sound play

AMI Features

intelligent

the system knows when it should remind you and how to do it.

sensitivity

the system is sensitive to the speed and distance between user and the back moving object like vehicle and people

responsive

once there is the vehicle or the people get inside the save region of user the bike will do the feedback of vibration to the user and light, sound to the other people, and when it's time to turn the road the bike will instruct you to do it.

transparent

the system is invisible to the user
